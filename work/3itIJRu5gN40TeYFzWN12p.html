One Snooze Away

One Snooze Away is here to make sure you never sleep through your stop again. Simply search for your destination, set an alarm, and take a nap. We'll wake you up when the bus is approaching your stop. One Snooze Away was created by two devs as a part of a one-day, MEAN Hackathon. I took on a back-end role and handled the Node.js/Express.js routing, MongoDB implementation, and Angular.js logic.
